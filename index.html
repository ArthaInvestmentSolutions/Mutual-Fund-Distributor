Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about mutual fund investments, SIP plans, PMS, and financial planning in India.

What is the best mutual fund to invest in India?

The best mutual fund depends on your risk appetite and time horizon. We recommend a blend of large-cap, flexi-cap, and ELSS funds for long-term wealth creation. Contact us for a free, personalised recommendation.

How do I start a SIP investment plan?

You can start a SIP with as little as ₹500/month. We help you choose the right mutual fund scheme, set up your systematic investment plan, and review performance — at no extra advisory cost.

What is PMS (Portfolio Management Service)?

PMS is a SEBI-regulated professional investment service for HNI investors (minimum ₹50 lakhs). A portfolio manager actively manages your equity portfolio for potentially higher, risk-adjusted returns.

Is Artha Investment Solutions SEBI/AMFI registered?

Yes. Artha Investment Solutions is an AMFI-Registered Mutual Fund Distributor (ARN-337744) with NISM Series 5A & 21A certifications — fully compliant with SEBI and AMFI regulations.

Which is better — ELSS, PPF or NPS for tax saving?

ELSS offers the shortest lock-in (3 years) with potential for higher equity-linked returns. PPF provides guaranteed, tax-free returns. NPS is ideal for retirement. We help you decide based on your tax bracket and goals.

How much should I invest in mutual funds monthly?

A good starting rule: invest at least 20% of your monthly income via SIP. We build a customised SIP plan aligned to your income, goals, and risk profile — whether you start at ₹1,000 or ₹1 lakh per month.
