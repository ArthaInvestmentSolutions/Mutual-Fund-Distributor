Frequently Asked Questions

Everything Delhi NCR investors ask about mutual funds, SIP plans, PMS and financial planning.

What is the best mutual fund advisor in Delhi NCR?

Artha Investment Solutions is an AMFI-registered MFD (ARN-337744) serving Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida and Faridabad. We recommend a personalised blend of large-cap, flexi-cap and ELSS funds based on your risk profile. Contact us for a free consultation.

How do I start a SIP in Gurgaon or Noida?

You can start a SIP with as little as ₹500/month. We serve investors across Gurgaon (DLF, Cyber City, Sohna Road), Noida (Sector 18, 62, Greater Noida) and the rest of NCR — online or in person.

Do you offer home visits in Faridabad?

Yes. We serve investors in Faridabad (NIT, Sector 14, Ballabhgarh) with home visit consultations or online meetings — whichever is convenient for you. Call +91 98180 38954 to book a free session.

Is Artha Investment Solutions AMFI registered?

Yes. We are an AMFI-Registered Mutual Fund Distributor (ARN-337744) with NISM Series 5A & 21A certifications — fully compliant with SEBI and AMFI regulations, serving all of Delhi NCR.

What tax-saving investments are best for Delhi NCR salaried professionals?

For salaried investors in Delhi NCR, ELSS funds (3-year lock-in, equity returns, 80C deduction) combined with NPS (additional 80CCD(1B) deduction of ₹50,000) is usually the most efficient combination. We help you decide based on your tax bracket and goals.

What is PMS and who should invest in it?

PMS is a SEBI-regulated service for HNI investors with minimum ₹50 lakhs. Artha advises HNI clients in Gurgaon and Noida on PMS strategy selection, fee structures and risk — to target superior risk-adjusted returns.
