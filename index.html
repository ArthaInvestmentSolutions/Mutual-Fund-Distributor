What tax-saving investments are best for Delhi NCR salaried professionals?

For salaried investors in Delhi NCR, ELSS funds (3-year lock-in, equity returns, 80C deduction) combined with NPS (additional 80CCD(1B) deduction of ₹50,000) is usually the most efficient combination. We help you decide based on your tax bracket and goals.

PMS is a SEBI-regulated service for HNI investors with minimum ₹50 lakhs. Artha advises HNI clients in Gurgaon and Noida on PMS strategy selection, fee structures and risk — to target superior risk-adjusted returns.
